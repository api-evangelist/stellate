# Stellate Plans

Source: https://stellate.co/pricing

## Free Plan

- **Price:** $0/month
- **Requests:** Up to 100,000 requests/month
- **Retention:** 1 day
- **Seats:** Unlimited
- **Services:** Unlimited
- **Features:** GraphQL Metrics & Insights, Edge Caching, Rate Limiting
- **Notes:** Fair use policy applies. Requests above 100k pass through without caching or rate limiting.

## Business Plan

- **Price:** Starts at $249/month
- **Requests:** Unlimited (25 million metrics requests included)
- **Retention:** 30 days
- **Seats:** Unlimited
- **Services:** Unlimited
- **Features:**
  - GraphQL Metrics & Insights
  - Edge Caching (add-on: +$5/month)
  - Rate Limiting (add-on: +$5/month)
  - Data Processing Agreement
- **Overages:** $10/month per additional 1 million requests
- **Payment:** VISA, Mastercard, AMEX; wire transfer available

## Enterprise Plan

- **Price:** Custom (contact sales)
- **Requests:** Unlimited
- **Retention:** Custom
- **Features:**
  - Everything in Business
  - SLA guarantees
  - SSO (Single Sign-On)
  - Volume discounts
  - Custom agreements
  - White-glove onboarding
  - All services included

## General Notes

- Going over plan limits disables features but does not block requests.
- Plans can be cancelled at any time with downgrade to Free.
- All pricing is in US dollars.
- Enterprise customers may pay via wire transfer.
